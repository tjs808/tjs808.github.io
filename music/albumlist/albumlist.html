Since I Left You

This album was pieced together by two Australian DJs entirely from thousands upon thousands of samples. The result is a lush soundscape that can make you get lost inside a story you create yourself.

Heaven or Las Vegas

The "shoegaze" genre, perfected. It's maximalism without being overwhelming or distracting: every instrument, every sound fits in perfectly. One fact that'll really blow your mind after the first listen: It's in English. The whole thing.

Bloom

The first of many classics in Beach House'sdiscography, this album packs an emotional punch in a way few other albums can. Lyrics and melody operate in perfect synergy, and there's an amazing balance between all the sounds in the mix.
