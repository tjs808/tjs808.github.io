I began learning the violin at age six, through Heartstrings Studio in River Falls, Wisconsin. Heartstrings taught the Suzuki Method, which you can learn more abouton this Wikipedia page.

My favorite pieces to play usually come from the Romantic era of classical music, but I'm not averse to Baroque pieces if they're a little more uptempo and complex.
